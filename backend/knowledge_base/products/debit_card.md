# Debit Card Services — Union Bank of India

## How to Block a Debit Card
Customers have multiple options to block their card immediately to prevent unauthorized access:

### Method 1: Via SMS
Send an SMS typing `UBLOCK <Last 4 digits of Debit Card>` to **09223008486** from the registered mobile number.

### Method 2: Via Customer Care
Call the 24/7 toll-free numbers **1800-22-2244** / **1800-208-2244**. Select the debit card hotlisting option and follow the IVR instructions.

### Method 3: Via Union Ease Mobile App
1. Log in to the **Union Ease** app.
2. Navigate to **Pay & Transfer** and select **Debit Card**.
3. Choose **Manage Debit Card**, then select **Block/Unblock**.
4. Select the card and toggle it to **Block**.

### Method 4: Via Internet Banking
1. Log in to **UBI Net Banking**.
2. Go to **General Services** > **Service Request**.
3. Select **Block Debit Card**, enter your account and card details, and submit.

---

## How to Unblock a Debit Card

### Temporary Blocks
If the card was temporarily disabled or "switched off" using the **Manage Debit Card** feature on the **Union Ease** app or Net Banking, the customer can log back in and toggle the setting back to **Unblock**.

### Permanent Blocks (Hotlisted)
If a card was permanently hotlisted (e.g., reported lost or stolen via customer care or SMS), it **cannot** be unblocked. The customer must apply for a new debit card.
