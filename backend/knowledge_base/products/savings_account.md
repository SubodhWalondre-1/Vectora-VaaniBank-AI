# Savings Account — Union Bank of India

## Savings Account Interest Rate Slabs (As of April 20, 2026)
Union Bank of India calculates savings account interest on a daily product basis and credits it quarterly. The interest rates are structured as follows:

| Daily Balance Slab | Effective Interest Rate (% p.a.) |
| :--- | :---: |
| Up to ₹50 Lakhs | 2.50% |
| > ₹50 Lakhs to ₹50 Crores | 2.65% |
| > ₹50 Crores to ₹100 Crores | 2.75% |
| > ₹100 Crores to ₹500 Crores | 3.00% |
| > ₹500 Crores to ₹1,000 Crores | 3.50% |
| > ₹1,000 Crores to ₹1,500 Crores | 4.00% |
| > ₹1,500 Crores to ₹2,000 Crores | 4.10% |
| > ₹2,000 Crores to ₹3,000 Crores | 5.00% |
| Above ₹3,000 Crores | 6.25% |

## Account Types
1. Regular Savings Account — standard account for salaried/self-employed individuals.
2. BSBD Account (Basic Savings Bank Deposit) — zero minimum balance, no charges.
3. PMJDY Account (Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana) — zero balance, RuPay card, Rs. 1 lakh accident insurance, Rs. 10,000 overdraft after 6 months.
4. Senior Citizen Savings Account — higher interest rate, priority service.
5. Minor Account — for children below 18, operated by guardian.

## Minimum Balance Requirements
- Urban branch: Rs. 1,000 per month
- Semi-urban branch: Rs. 500 per month
- Rural branch: Rs. 500 per month
- BSBD / PMJDY account: Rs. 0 (zero balance, no penalty)

## Required Documents — All Account Types
1. Aadhaar card (mandatory for eKYC)
2. PAN card (mandatory; Form 60 if no PAN)
3. Recent passport size photograph (2 copies)
4. Address proof if Aadhaar address is different from current address

## Step-by-Step Account Opening Process
* **Step 1:** Ask customer which type of account they want (regular / BSBD / PMJDY).
* **Step 2:** Collect Aadhaar and PAN; verify originals.
* **Step 3:** Perform eKYC via Aadhaar OTP (takes 5 minutes with Aadhaar-linked mobile).
* **Step 4:** Customer fills account opening form (AOF).
* **Step 5:** Initial deposit: minimum Rs. 1,000 for regular; Rs. 0 for BSBD/PMJDY.
* **Step 6:** Account number generated immediately; passbook and debit card dispatched in 7 days.
