## Key Premium Rules
* **Senior Citizens (aged 60 and above):** Receive an additional premium of **0.50%** over the regular rate.
* **Super Senior Citizens (aged 80 and above):** Receive an additional premium of **0.75%** over the regular rate (which translates to a 0.25% premium on top of the base Senior Citizen rate).
* **Tax-Saving FD (5 to 10 years):** Fixed at **6.00%** for regular depositors and **6.50%** for senior citizens.

## TDS Rules (Tax Deducted at Source)
* TDS deducted if FD interest exceeds Rs. 40,000 in a financial year (Rs. 50,000 for senior citizens).
* TDS rate: 10% if PAN provided; 20% if PAN not provided.
* Form 15G (below 60 years) or Form 15H (60+ years) can be submitted to avoid TDS if total income is below taxable limit.

## Premature Withdrawal Rules
* Allowed anytime.
* Penalty: 1% reduction on applicable rate (calculated as the lower of the rate on booking for completed tenure or the original booked rate minus 1.00%).
* No penalty for FD held for less than 7 days (no interest paid).

## Step-by-Step FD Opening Process
* **Step 1:** Confirm customer has existing savings account (preferred) or open new account.
* **Step 2:** Check if PAN is on file; if FD interest likely to exceed Rs. 40,000 ask for Form 15G/15H.
* **Step 3:** Fill FD application: amount, tenure, payout option (cumulative or quarterly interest payout).
* **Step 4:** Debit from savings account; FD receipt generated immediately.
* **Step 5:** Advise nomination and auto-renewal preference.
