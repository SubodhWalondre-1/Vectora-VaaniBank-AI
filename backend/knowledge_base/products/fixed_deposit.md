# Fixed Deposit (FD) and Recurring Deposit (RD) — Union Bank of India

## Fixed Deposit Parameters
- Minimum deposit: Rs. 1,000
- Maximum deposit: No upper limit
- Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
- Interest rate: 3.00% to 7.25% per annum depending on tenure
- Senior citizen additional rate: +0.50% per annum over regular rate

## FD Interest Rate Slab (indicative, subject to change)
- 7 days to 14 days: 3.00%
- 15 days to 45 days: 3.50%
- 46 days to 90 days: 4.50%
- 91 days to 180 days: 5.50%
- 181 days to 364 days: 6.25%
- 1 year to 2 years: 6.80%
- 2 years to 3 years: 7.00%
- 3 years to 5 years: 6.75%
- 5 years to 10 years: 6.50%

## TDS Rules (Tax Deducted at Source)
- TDS deducted if FD interest exceeds Rs. 40,000 in a financial year (Rs. 50,000 for senior citizens)
- TDS rate: 10% if PAN provided; 20% if PAN not provided
- Form 15G (below 60 years) or Form 15H (60+ years) can be submitted to avoid TDS if total income is below taxable limit
- PAN is mandatory to avoid 20% TDS

## Required Documents
1. Existing account holder: only FD amount and tenure needed; KYC already on file
2. New customer: Aadhaar + PAN + photograph (same as account opening)

## Recurring Deposit (RD) Parameters
- Minimum monthly instalment: Rs. 100
- Maximum: No upper limit
- Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
- Interest rate: Same as FD for corresponding tenure
- Penalty for missed instalment: Rs. 1.50 per Rs. 100 per month

## Premature Withdrawal Rules
- Allowed anytime
- Penalty: 1% reduction on applicable rate
- No penalty for FD held for less than 7 days (no interest paid)

## Auto-Renewal
- Default: FD auto-renews at maturity for same tenure at prevailing rate
- Customer can change: maturity proceeds to savings account

## Step-by-Step FD Opening Process
Step 1: Confirm customer has existing savings account (preferred) or open new account
Step 2: Check if PAN is on file; if FD interest likely to exceed Rs. 40,000 ask for Form 15G/15H
Step 3: Fill FD application: amount, tenure, payout option (cumulative or quarterly interest payout)
Step 4: Debit from savings account; FD receipt generated immediately
Step 5: Advise nomination and auto-renewal preference
