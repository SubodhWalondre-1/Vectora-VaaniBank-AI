# Banking Glossary — Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu equivalents

## Loan Terms
- EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment) | Hindi: saman masik kist | Marathi: saman masik haft | Tamil: சம மாத தவணை | Telugu: సమాన నెలవారీ వాయిదా
- Interest rate | Hindi: byaj dar | Marathi: vyaj dar | Tamil: வட்டி விகிதம் | Telugu: వడ్డీ రేటు
- Principal | Hindi: mool rashi | Marathi: mool rakkam | Tamil: அசல் தொகை | Telugu: అసలు మొత్తం
- Collateral / Security | Hindi: zamanat | Marathi: taranasa | Tamil: பிணை | Telugu: హామీ
- Guarantor | Hindi: zamaanat-daar | Marathi: jaameendar | Tamil: ஜாமீன்காரர் | Telugu: జామీనుదారుడు
- Tenure | Hindi: avadhi | Marathi: mudat | Tamil: காலம் | Telugu: వ్యవధి
- Foreclosure | Hindi: purv-bhugtan | Marathi: agau pherphad | Tamil: முன்கூட்டிய தீர்வு | Telugu: ముందస్తు తీర్పు
- Processing fee | Hindi: prakriya shulk | Marathi: prakrya shulk | Tamil: செயலாக்க கட்டணம் | Telugu: ప్రాసెసింగ్ ఫీజు

- Current account | Hindi: chal khata | Marathi: chal khat | Tamil: நடப்பு கணக்கு | Telugu: కరెంట్ ఖాతా
- Fixed Deposit | Hindi: miaadi jama | Marathi: mudat thev | Tamil: நிலையான வைப்பு | Telugu: స్థిర డిపాజిట్
- Passbook | Hindi: bachat pustika | Marathi: khat pustika | Tamil: பாஸ்புக் | Telugu: పాస్ బుక్
- Cheque book | Hindi: cheque pustak | Marathi: cheque pustak | Tamil: காசோலை புத்தகம் | Telugu: చెక్కు పుస్తకం
- Balance | Hindi: bakaya / shesh | Marathi: shillak | Tamil: இருப்பு | Telugu: నిల్వ
- Nomination | Hindi: namankeet | Marathi: namankrut | Tamil: நியமனம் | Telugu: నామినీ

## KYC Terms
- Know Your Customer (KYC) | Hindi: grahak parichay | Marathi: grahak parichay | Tamil: வாடிக்கையாளர் அடையாளம் | Telugu: కస్టమర్ గుర్తింపు
- Aadhaar seeding | Hindi: Aadhaar jodna | Marathi: Aadhaar jodne | Tamil: ஆதார் இணைப்பு | Telugu: ఆధార్ లింక్
- Address proof | Hindi: pata praman | Marathi: pata pura | Tamil: முகவரி சான்று | Telugu: చిరునామా నిరూపణ

## Banking Schemes
- PMJDY (Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana) | Hindi: Jan Dhan Yojana | zero balance account scheme for financial inclusion
- PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana) | Hindi: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana | home loan subsidy for EWS/LIG/MIG categories
- Mudra Loan | Hindi: Mudra rin | small business loan up to Rs. 10 lakh; sub-categories: Shishu (up to 50K), Kishore (50K to 5L), Tarun (5L to 10L)
- DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) | Hindi: seedha laabh hastaantaran | government subsidy directly to Aadhaar-linked bank account
- NEFT | Hindi: NEFT | National Electronic Funds Transfer; settlement in batches; available 24x7
- RTGS | Hindi: RTGS | Real Time Gross Settlement; for amounts above Rs. 2 lakh; real-time settlement
- IMPS | Hindi: IMPS | Immediate Payment Service; instant 24x7 transfer; up to Rs. 5 lakh per transaction

## Regulatory Terms
- CIBIL score | Hindi: CIBIL ank | credit score from TransUnion CIBIL; range 300-900; 700+ considered good
- RBI | Hindi: Bhartiya Reserve Bank | Reserve Bank of India; central bank and regulator
- NPA (Non Performing Asset) | Hindi: akaryakari sampati | loan where EMI not paid for 90+ days
- NACH | Hindi: NACH | National Automated Clearing House; used for auto-debit of EMI
