# Personal Loan — Union Bank of India

## Eligibility
- Minimum CIBIL score: 700
- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Salaried government/PSU employees preferred; private sector also eligible
- Minimum monthly net salary: Rs. 20,000
- Minimum 1 year of continuous employment with current employer

## Loan Parameters
- Minimum amount: Rs. 50,000
- Maximum amount: Rs. 15 lakh
- Tenure: 12 to 60 months
- Interest rate: Starts at 8.75% p.a.
- Processing fee: 1.00% (min Rs. 500, max Rs. 7,500). Pensioners: NIL.
- No collateral or security required (clean loan)

## EMI Reference (approximate at 11% per annum)
- 1 lakh for 12 months: approx Rs. 8,838/month
- 2 lakh for 24 months: approx Rs. 9,301/month
- 5 lakh for 36 months: approx Rs. 16,370/month
- 10 lakh for 48 months: approx Rs. 25,850/month
- 15 lakh for 60 months: approx Rs. 32,600/month

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar card
2. PAN card (mandatory)
3. Last 3 months salary slips
4. Last 3 months bank statement
5. Latest Form 16 or ITR
6. Employment ID card or offer letter
7. Passport size photograph (2 copies)

## Step-by-Step Process
Step 1: Check CIBIL score — minimum 700 required
Step 2: Calculate eligibility — maximum EMI should not exceed 40% of net monthly salary
Step 3: Collect documents and fill application form
Step 4: Credit appraisal by branch (2-3 working days)
Step 5: Disbursement directly to salary account

## Key Rules
- Existing loan EMIs are deducted before calculating eligibility
- Foreclosure allowed after 6 EMIs; foreclosure charge: 2% of outstanding principal
- Maximum 2 personal loans at same time permitted
- Salary account with Union Bank speeds up processing
