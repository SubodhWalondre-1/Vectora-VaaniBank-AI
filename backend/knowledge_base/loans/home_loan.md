# Home Loan — Union Bank of India

## EBLR and Interest Rate Slabs (As of April 2026)
* **EBLR (External Benchmark Lending Rate):** 8.00% p.a. (calculated as RBI Repo Rate of 5.25% + Spread of 2.75%).
* **CIBIL Score Discount Matrix (Salaried / Professional Male under Union Home Scheme):**
  * **800 & above:** EBLR - 0.85% = **7.15% p.a.**
  * **750 to 799:** EBLR - 0.65% = **7.35% p.a.**
  * **700 to 749:** EBLR - 0.25% = **7.75% p.a.**
  * **680 to 699:** No discount (EBLR) = **8.00% p.a.**
  * **650 to 679:** EBLR + 0.85% = **8.85% p.a.**
  * **600 to 649:** EBLR + 1.35% = **9.35% p.a.**

## Key Rate Variations
* **Women Applicants:** Receive a 0.05% concession across all slabs (e.g., 7.10% for an 800+ CIBIL). The property must be singly or jointly owned by the female borrower to qualify.
* **Non-Salaried Applicants:** Face an additional premium of 0.05% to 0.10% over the salaried rates.
* **Government/PSU Employees:** Benefit from a flat rate of 7.15% for any score of 750 and above.

## Processing Fees, Prepayment Rules, and LTV Caps
* **Processing Fee:** 0.50% of the sanctioned loan amount.
* **Maximum Fee Cap:** Capped at a maximum of ₹15,000 + GST.
* **Prepayment Charges:** NIL for all floating-rate home loans. Borrowers can prepay or foreclose without penalties.
* **Loan-to-Value (LTV) Caps:**
  * **Loans up to ₹30 Lakh:** Up to 90% of the property value.
  * **Loans from ₹30 Lakh to ₹75 Lakh:** Up to 80% of the property value.
  * **Loans above ₹75 Lakh:** Up to 75% of the property value.

## Maximum Repayment Tenures
* **Primary Home Loan:** Maximum repayment tenure is 30 years (or until the borrower reaches the maximum exit age, which is generally 75 years at loan maturity).
* **Top-Up Loans (Union Mortgage Plus):** Priced at 0.75% above your base home loan rate. Repayment tenure for top-ups generally aligns with the outstanding tenure of the primary home loan.

## Required Documents — Salaried
1. Aadhaar card (original + self-attested copy)
2. PAN card (mandatory, RBI rule)
3. Last 3 months salary slips
4. Last 6 months bank statement (salary account)
5. Form 16 for last 2 years
6. Property documents: sale deed, NOC from builder/society
7. Property valuation report (from bank-approved valuer)
8. Passport size photograph (2 copies)
9. Employment proof: offer letter or company ID card

## Required Documents — Self Employed
1. Aadhaar card
2. PAN card (mandatory)
3. ITR for last 2 years with computation
4. CA-certified profit and loss statement
5. Business registration certificate
6. Last 12 months bank statement (current account)
7. Property documents: same as salaried

## Step-by-Step Process
* **Step 1:** Verify CIBIL score — if below 650, application generally not processed.
* **Step 2:** Collect income proof and check eligibility (max loan = approx 60x monthly income).
* **Step 3:** Property shortlisting — confirm LTV does not exceed threshold (90%/80%/75%).
* **Step 4:** Submit application form with KYC documents.
* **Step 5:** Bank legal and technical verification of property (7-10 working days).
* **Step 6:** Sanction letter issued after approval.
* **Step 7:** Loan disbursement after property registration.
